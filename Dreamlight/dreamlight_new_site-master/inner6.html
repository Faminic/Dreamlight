About Dreamlight

Dreamlight are a sleep brand for people who want technology to improve their sleep, not disrupt it.

By reinventing the classic sleep mask, we have created a wearable that lasts. Traditional methods and new techniques merge to form a timeless, understated products.

Since launching in 2017, we have launched to worldwide critical acclaim via crowdfunding. Recently, expanding our business to include Amazon and direct online sales.
